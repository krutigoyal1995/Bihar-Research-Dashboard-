Scholarship Utilization & Policy
Positive Driver
Strict 75% attendance rule → children attend MORE to stay eligible. Scholarship + mid-day meal together = strongest attendance combo. But Aadhaar/bank errors stall payments for entire years.
Key Findings
✓Mid-day meal + scholarship = children attend regularly and happily
✓When bank account opened and money flows, child highly motivated
✗Bank account goes dormant → money returns to government; family unaware
✗Aadhaar spelling errors → repeated block visits; one error = whole year withheld
✗Teachers reportedly charge money to fill scholarship forms
💡Recommended: Centralize process via school with disbursement feedback loop to school
Teachers recommend ₹5,000–₹7,000/yr
